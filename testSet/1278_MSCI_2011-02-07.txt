The 2-year to 30-year yield curve narrowed five basis points\nto 3.94 percentage points at 5:02 p.m. in New York, after the\nspread hit a record high of 4.02 percent on Feb. 1, according to\nBGCantor Market Data.  10-Year Notes  Yields on 10-year notes increased one basis point, or 0.01\npercentage point, to 3.63 percent. On\nFeb. 9 it plans to buy $6 billion to $8 billion of Treasuries\nmaturing between February 2015 and July 2016.
