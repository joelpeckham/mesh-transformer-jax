Prior to his appointment as CEO, Wilson was chief operating officer and executive vice president, and before that he was president of Varian Medical\u2019s Oncology Systems business.\nWilson also had a 19-year career with GE Healthcare, culminating in his position as CEO of GE\u2019s Healthcare Information Technology unit.\n\u201cDow brings an important breadth of experience in the healthcare industry. I look forward to working with him.\u201d\nWilson is also a member of the Board of Directors of AdvaMed, a U.S. trade association representing 80 percent of the medical technology firms in the U.S., and Varex Imaging Corporation, which was created through the successful spin-off of Varian Medical. His term on the Varex board naturally ends in February 2018. Formerly, Wilson was lead independent director of Saba Software, Inc.\nIn 2014, he was appointed by then-U.S. Secretary of Commerce, Penny Pritzker, to the newly formed President\u2019s Advisory Council on Doing Business in Africa.\nWilson received an MBA from the Tuck School of Business at Dartmouth College, New Hampshire and a Bachelor of Arts degree from Brigham Young University, Utah.\n\u201cI have long admired Agilent\u2019s scientific solutions, as well as its strong customer focus and impeccable reputation for integrity.
