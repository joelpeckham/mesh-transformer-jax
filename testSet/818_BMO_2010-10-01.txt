It touched C$1.0188, the strongest level since Aug.\n6. The loonie strengthened 0.3 percent for the past five days,\ncapping the longest streak of weekly gains since the period\nended May 8, 2009. One Canadian dollar buys 97.99 U.S. cents.  Crude oil, Canada\u2019s biggest export, rose to a seven-week\nhigh after Chinese and U.S. economic data bolstered optimism\ndemand is growing in the world\u2019s two largest consumers of the\nfuel.
