At the CES 2016, Dr. Hong plans to provide a look into Samsung\u2019s progress in turning the vision of the IoT into reality, focusing on tangible solutions and products in areas most closely related to our everyday lives, with specific emphases on Samsung's IoT data security and customer-centric mobile payment solutions. \u201cWe are thrilled to welcome Dr. WP Hong, an established IT and mobile industry leader, to the keynote stage at CES 2016,\u201d said Gary Shapiro, President and CEO, CEA. \u201cWith an array of products including televisions, mobile phones, tablets and much more, Samsung amazes at each CES with its innovative products spanning the entire tech ecosystem, and we look forward to hearing Dr. Hong\u2019s expert opinion.\u201d Before assuming his current position, Dr. Hong contributed to establishing Samsung\u2019s GALAXY franchise as a synonym for its popular and advanced Android products. As the head of global product strategy, Dr. Hong oversaw Samsung\u2019s transformation from a mass market volume player to a leader in sophisticated mobile products. In addition to his work on the Android brand, he was a driving force behind Samsung\u2019s innovations in IoT, multi-screen consumer experiences, cloud storage, music, video, e-book and game services. Prior to joining Samsung, he accumulated experience in the mobile industry both as a researcher and business person on the forefront of mobile technology innovations, both in the U.S. and Korea. He holds a master\u2019s degree and Ph.D. from the University of Michigan. Dr. Hong joins Intel\u2019s CEO, Brian Krzanich, on the CES 2016 keynote stage.
