The body of the article is as follows:\n    \nTOMI(TM) Receives $8 Million Equity Investment July 08, 2015 16:05 ET | Source: TOMI Environmental Solutions, Inc. \nBEVERLY HILLS, Calif., July 8,   TOMI\u2122 Environmental Solutions, Inc. (OTCQB:TOMZ) ( TOMI ), a global bacteria decontamination and infection prevention company, announced that it has received an equity investment of $8 million from Arise Asset Management Private Limited (Arise). As part of the transaction, TOMI issued approximately 17 million shares of restricted common stock to Arise. Established in 2001, Arise is a private and public wealth management fund based in Singapore that focuses primarily on publicly traded technology companies around the world. \nDr. Halden Shane, TOMI's Chief Executive Officer, stated: \"This equity transaction demonstrates our commitment to improving our balance sheet and positioning the company for long-term growth. A portion of the proceeds of this capital raise enabled us to retire approximately $5 million in convertible debt plus interest, resulting in TOMI becoming completely debt-free. Following last month's announcement that the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) registered TOMI's SteraMist\u2122 with Binary Ionization Technology\u00ae (BIT\u2122) for use in hospitals and healthcare facilities, we believe that we are very well positioned to bring our leading, cutting-edge SteraMist\u2122 technology to market in rapid fashion.\" \nAbout TOMI \u2122 Environmental Solutions, Inc. \nTOMI \u2122 Environmental Solutions, Inc. (OTCQB:TOMZ) is a global bacteria decontamination and infectious disease control company, providing eco-friendly environmental solutions for indoor surface decontamination through manufacturing, sales and licensing of our premier platform of Hydrogen Peroxide based products that uses Binary Ionization Technology\u00ae (BIT \u2122 ), a state of the art technology for the production of its healthcare-hospital disinfectant mist represented by the TOMI\u2122 SteraMist\u2122 brand. \nTOMI's products are designed to service a broad spectrum of commercial structures including hospitals and medical facilities, cruise ships, office buildings, hotel and motel rooms, schools, restaurants, for non-food safety in meat and produce processing facilities, military barracks, and athletic facilities. TOMI's products and services have also been used in single-family homes and multi-unit residences. \nTOMI also develops training programs and application protocols for its clients and is a member in good standing with The American Biological Safety Association, The American Association of Tissue Banks, Association for Professionals in Infection Control and Epidemiology, Society for Healthcare Epidemiology of America, The Restoration Industry Association, Indoor Air Quality Association, and The International Ozone Association.
