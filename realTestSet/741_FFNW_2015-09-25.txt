Announces New Branch of Subsidiary Bank September 25, 2015 09:30 ET | Source: First Financial Northwest RENTON, Wash., Sept. 25,   First Financial Northwest, Inc. (\u201cCompany\u201d) (NASDAQ: FFNW ) today announced that its subsidiary financial institution, First Financial Northwest Bank (\u201cBank\u201d), has opened a branch office in Mill Creek, Washington. The Mill Creek office is staffed with a local, successful, seasoned, and highly-skilled team. The Bank\u2019s expansion into Snohomish County brings a continued focus on efficiency with a small footprint and an extensive use of the latest banking technology. The office is located in the popular Mill Creek Town Center at 15021 Main St., Suite F, Mill Creek, WA 98012, where the Bank is able to connect with its new community. Well-known for the personalized service that it has provided to its banking customers over the years in King County, Snohomish County will now enjoy this service along with the Bank\u2019s complete range of financial solutions designed to meet customers' individual, family, and business banking needs, and its dedication to continue to update its unique financial solutions. The Bank has also received regulatory approval to open its next branch office in Edmonds, Washington. It anticipates this new branch will open in early 2016. For more information about the Bank\u2019s products and services, visit www.ffnwb.com . \nAbout the Company and the Bank First Financial Northwest, Inc. is the parent company of the Bank; a Washington chartered stock savings bank headquartered in Renton, Washington.
