Mr. van Ratingen succeeds Mr. Daniel Daly, who will retire on July 31, 2015. In his role as Country President, Mr. van Ratingen will oversee the operations and business development activities of ACE\u2019s key business lines in the Philippines namely, Property & Casualty, Accident & Health and Personal & Business Insurance. He is also responsible for the management of regulatory relationships, strategic partnerships and affinity relationships. He will report to Juan Luis Ortega, Regional President for ACE\u2019s general insurance operations in Asia Pacific. Mr. van Ratingen brings with him more than 20 years of industry experience from a career stretching a number of senior management roles across both Asia and Europe. His career includes more than 13 years with CIGNA International, where his last position was Chief Operating Officer for CIGNA\u2019s European Life, Accident & Health Division. Prior to joining ACE, he was the Regional General Manager at a boutique consultancy specializing in the financial services sector which provided strategic counsel to clients on direct marketing, sales as well as customer acquisition and retention in Asia Pacific. He joined ACE in 2009 as Country President for Indonesia, and was instrumental in growing the general insurance operation. His most recent role was Asia Pacific\u2019s Regional Head of Bancassurance where he helped accelerate the expansion of this distribution channel by establishing a number of bank partnerships for ACE to access a wider range of customers.
