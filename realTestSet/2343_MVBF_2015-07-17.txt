Announces Completion of Stock Offering and Related Conversion July 16, 2015 17:00 ET | Source: MSB Financial Corp. \nMILLINGTON, N.J., July 16, 2015 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- MSB Financial Corp., a Maryland corporation (the \u201cCompany\u201d) (Nasdaq: MSBF ), announced today that it has completed its stock offering and the related conversion from the mutual holding company to the stock holding company form of organization. As a result of the closing of the conversion and stock offering, the Company is now the holding company for Millington Savings Bank (the \u201cBank\u201d). MSB Financial Corp. (\u201cMSB-Federal\u201d) and MSB Financial, MHC, the Bank\u2019s former mutual holding company, have ceased to exist. The results of the stock offering were previously reported in MSB-Federal\u2019s press release dated July 15, 2015. \nBeginning on Friday, July 17, 2015, the shares of Company common stock are expected to trade on the Nasdaq Global Market under the trading symbol MSBF. \nSubscribers can confirm their allocation by contacting the Stock Information Center at (844) 265-9680 from Monday through Friday, 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Eastern Time. Alternatively, subscribers may confirm allocations online, at https://allocations.kbw.com. \nBook entry statements reflecting shares purchased in the offering are expected to be mailed to subscribers promptly following the closing. \nAs a result of the conversion, each existing share of MSB-Federal common stock has been converted into the right to receive 1.1397 shares of Company common stock. Stockholders of MSB-Federal holding shares in street name will automatically receive shares of Company common stock within their accounts. Stockholders of MSB-Federal holding shares in certificated form will receive book entry statements for their shares of Company common stock through the Company\u2019s Direct Registration System, after returning their stock certificates with a properly completed letter of transmittal to the Company\u2019s transfer agent. Letters of transmittal will be promptly sent out to these registered stockholders by the Company\u2019s transfer agent. Cash will be paid in lieu of any fractional shares based on the sale price in the offering of $10.00 per share. Approximately 5,953,834 shares of Company common stock will be outstanding after the completion of the offering and the exchange, before taking into account adjustments for fractional shares. \nKeefe, Bruyette & Woods, Inc. (\u201cKBW\u201d) acted as financial advisor for the Company in connection with the offering. Jones Walker LLP acted as legal counsel to the Company in connection with the conversion and offering.
