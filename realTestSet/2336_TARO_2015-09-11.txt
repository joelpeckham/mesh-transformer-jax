Indian companies had invested $1.38 billion in their overseas ventures in August 2014. In July 2015, Indian companies had invested $1.23 billion in overseas markets. The investments were a mix of issuance of guarantees ($1.83 billion), loan ($177.19 million) and of equity ($184.08 million).
