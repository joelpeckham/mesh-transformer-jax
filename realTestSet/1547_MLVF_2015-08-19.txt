The body of the article is as follows:\n    \nMalvern Federal Savings Bank Enters New Jersey Market; Appoints Banking Veteran William Boylan as Head of Its New Jersey Commercial Lending Division August 19, 2015 12:12 ET | Source: Malvern Bancorp, Inc. PAOLI, Pa., Aug. 19,   Malvern Federal Savings Bank (the \u201cBank\u201d or \u201cMalvern Federal\u201d), the wholly owned subsidiary of Malvern Bancorp, Inc. (the \u201cCompany\u201d) (NASDAQ: MLVF ), today announced it has entered the commercial banking market in New Jersey with the appointment of veteran banker William J. Boylan as head of Malvern Federal\u2019s new New Jersey Commercial Lending Division. \n\u201cBill brings more than 30 years of New Jersey banking experience to Malvern Federal and is the ideal individual to introduce our bank to the New Jersey market,\u201d said Anthony C. Weagley, President and CEO of Malvern Bancorp, Inc., and Malvern Federal Savings Bank. \u201cHe is highly regarded in the state as a commercial lender and leader of mortgage, consumer lending and business banking sales teams. Under Bill\u2019s leadership, I expect to quickly grow the Malvern Federal brand in the Garden State.\u201d \nPrior to joining Malvern Federal, Bill was senior vice president, commercial lending for ConnectOne Bank, formerly Union Center National Bank, in Union, N.J. He previously served as senior vice president, business development and marketing for both Northern State Bank in Closter, N.J., and NVE Bank in Englewood, N.J. He also served in management positions in the Fort Lee, N.J., and Englewood offices of Summit Bank/United Jersey Bank. \nBill is a past chairman of the Mortgage Bankers Association of New Jersey\u2019s Commercial Property Division; a former member and past president of the Englewood Rotary Club; and former director of the Englewood Chamber of Commerce. He currently serves as the immediate past chairman of the board of trustees and a trustee of Vantage Health System, a non-profit mental health organization serving Bergen County. \nAbout Malvern Bancorp \nMalvern Bancorp, Inc. is the holding company for Malvern Federal Savings Bank. Malvern Federal Savings Bank is a federally-chartered, FDIC-insured savings bank that was originally organized in 1887 and now serves as one of the oldest banks headquartered on the Philadelphia Mainline. For more than a century, Malvern Federal has been committed to helping people build prosperous communities as a trusted financial partner, forging lasting relationships through teamwork, respect and integrity. The Bank conducts business from its headquarters in Paoli, Pennsylvania, a suburb of Philadelphia, as well as seven other financial centers located throughout Chester and Delaware Counties, Pennsylvania. Its primary market niche is providing personalized service to its client base. The Bank focuses its lending activities on retail clients, commercial lending to small and medium-sized businesses, real estate developers and high net worth individuals. \nFor further information regarding Malvern Bancorp, Inc., please visit our web site at http://ir.malvernfederal.com .
